Definitions

Attack
An action which can be used to make one melee or ranged attack.
Cast a Spell
An action used to cast a new spell, or continue casting an channeled spell. Most spells require a casting time of 1 action.
Dash
An action which increases your movement distance this turn by your speed. In most situations this will grant you double movement.
Disengage
An action which prevents your movement from provoking opportunity attacks for the rest of the turn.
Dodge
An action which increases your chance of avoiding attacks. Attack rolls made against you suffer disadvantage if you can see the attacker. You make **Dexterity** saving throws with advantage. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn or if you become **incapacitated**.
Help
An action used to assist another creature in a task. The creature gains advantage on the next ability check or attack roll before your next turn.
Hide
This action allows you to make a **Dexterity (Stealth)** check to hide. This check is opposed by either an active or passive **Wisdom (Perception)** check depending on if the viewer is looking actively or not. The hidden state lasts until you are discovered or you stop hiding. Hidden creatures gain advantage on attack rolls against creatures that can't see it.
Ready
This action allows you to prepare a separate action which will be sprung based on a trigger you choose.
Search
An action during which you devote your attention to finding something. The DM might make you perform a **Wisdom (Perception)** or **Intelligence (Investigation)** check.
Use an Object
An action which allows you to interact with an object. You can use your weapon for free during an attack. This action is reserved for more advanced interaction or multiple interactions.
